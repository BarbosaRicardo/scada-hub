{\small\sffamily\color{pgray}\MakeUppercase{Course Topics}}
\smallskip

\setlist[enumerate]{noitemsep, topsep=2pt, leftmargin=*, label=\textcolor{pblue}{\small\bfseries\arabic*.}}
\begin{enumerate}\small
  \item \textbf{Introduction:} Feedback control concept and the uniform mathematical structure underlying temperature, pressure, flow, and level applications.
  \item \textbf{Control Loop Fundamentals:} Process variable, setpoint, error, and manipulated variable — vocabulary prerequisite for PID algorithm study.
  \item \textbf{PID Algorithm:} Proportional, integral, and derivative terms; the specific problem each term solves; combined three-term controller behavior.
  \item \textbf{Tuning Methods:} Ziegler-Nichols closed-loop and open-loop, lambda tuning, IMC; method selection criteria and failure modes.
  \item \textbf{Process Characterization:} First-order plus dead time model, step test procedure, and extracting gain, time constant, and dead time from equipment.
  \item \textbf{Cascade Control:} Inner and outer loop design, commissioning sequence, and disturbance rejection improvement over single-loop control.
  \item \textbf{Digital Implementation:} Sampling rate, velocity-form vs.\ position-form algorithm, bumpless transfer, and anti-windup on saturated outputs.
  \item \textbf{PLC Implementation:} PID function block configuration across Rockwell, Siemens, Beckhoff, and Modicon; scaling conventions; commissioning procedure.
  \item \textbf{Troubleshooting:} Oscillation, offset, slow response, and derivative chatter — each symptom mapped to a specific tuning adjustment.
  \item \textbf{Practice Lab:} Flow loop step-test tuning, cascade temperature/flow loop, anti-windup configuration, cold-start commissioning sequence.
\end{enumerate}

\medskip
{\color{pnavy}\rule{\linewidth}{0.4pt}}
\smallskip
{\small\sffamily\color{pgray}\MakeUppercase{Learning Outcomes}}
\smallskip

\setlist[itemize]{noitemsep, topsep=2pt, leftmargin=*, label=\textcolor{pblue}{\small$\bullet$}}
\begin{itemize}\small
  \item Perform a step test on live equipment and extract first-order plus dead time model parameters.
  \item Apply Ziegler-Nichols and lambda tuning methods to self-regulating process loops.
  \item Design and commission a cascade control loop with sequenced inner and outer tuning.
  \item Configure anti-windup and bumpless transfer on a PLC PID function block.
  \item Diagnose oscillation, steady-state offset, and derivative chatter from trend data alone.
\end{itemize}
